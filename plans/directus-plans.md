# Directus Plans

Source: https://directus.com/pricing

## Core Plan

- **Price:** Free ($0/month)
- **User Seats:** 3 user seats
- **Collections:** 25 Collections
- **Flows (Automation):** 5 Flows
- **Features:**
  - AI Assistant
  - Advanced RBAC (Role-Based Access Control)
  - REST and GraphQL APIs
  - Community Support
- **Cloud Hosting:** Optional add-on
- **Notes:** Suitable for individuals and small projects. Self-hosting is always free with no feature limits.

## Team Plan

- **Price:** $499/month (billed annually) or $599/month (billed monthly)
- **User Seats:** 10 SSO seats
- **Collections:** 50 Collections
- **Flows (Automation):** 20 Flows
- **Features:**
  - Everything in Core
  - Granular RBAC
  - SSO (Single Sign-On)
  - Basic Support
- **Cloud Hosting:** Optional add-on
- **Notes:** Designed for growing teams needing more capacity and SSO integration.

## Enterprise Plan

- **Price:** Custom pricing (contact sales)
- **User Seats:** 50+ SSO seats
- **Collections:** 200+ Collections
- **Flows (Automation):** Unlimited
- **Features:**
  - Everything in Team
  - SAML / OIDC SSO
  - Custom LLM integration
  - Offline Mode
  - AI Translations
  - Telemetry Opt-out
  - Dedicated support
- **Cloud Hosting:** Available (pricing custom)
- **Notes:** Tailored for large organizations. Volume discounts available for non-profits, bulk licenses, and partners.

## Open Innovation Grant

- **Price:** Free (self-hosted) or $99/month (cloud hosted)
- **Eligibility:** Organizations with under $5M annual revenue and fewer than 50 employees
- **Features:** Full permissive access with no feature limits
- **Notes:** Designed to support open-source projects, startups, and smaller organizations.

## Self-Hosted (Open Source)

- **Price:** Free forever (Apache 2.0 license)
- **Limits:** No artificial limitations — all features available
- **Support:** Community forum and Discord
- **Notes:** Deploy on any infrastructure. Enterprise Self-Hosted pricing is available for organizations needing commercial support, SLAs, or additional features.

## General Notes

- All cloud plans include the REST and GraphQL APIs.
- Self-hosted deployments have no seat, collection, or flow limits.
- Enterprise Self-Hosted pricing is negotiated per project with custom SLAs.
- Discounts available for non-profits, open-source projects, and volume licenses.
